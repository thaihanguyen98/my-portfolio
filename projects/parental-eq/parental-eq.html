Parental EQ

Empowering Parents Through Emotional Intelligence

ParentalEQ is a digital platform designed to help parents develop self-awareness, confidence, and deeper connections with their children. The platform offers structured modules, interactive activities, and guided exercises to enhance parent-child relationships by fostering emotional intelligence.

My role:

As a UI Designer, I was responsible for improving the user interface, user experience, and overall visual design of the product. Collaborating with the CEO, CFO, COO, and another UI designer, I worked in an agile environment to refine the platform through workshops, iterative design, and content strategy.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Conducted comparative and competitive research to identify industry best practices
  • Developed user flows to streamline the user journey
  • Created sketches, wireframes, and prototypes to visualize design improvements
  • Led content strategy to ensure clarity and engagement
  • Facilitated user testing to validate design decisions
