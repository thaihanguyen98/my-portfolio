Parental EQ

Enhancing Emotional Intelligence for Parents

ParentalEQ is a parenting content platform that is designed for parents to be more self-aware, confident and connected with their children. This platform involves modules, activities and other exercises to build a new sense of understanding between parents and children, improving their relationship.

My role:

I was contracted as a UI designer to improve better user interface and user experience, as well as the look and feel of the product. I worked collaborating with the CEO, CFO, COO and a UI designer for workshops, iteration and content strategy in an agile environment.

I was responsible for:

  • Comparative/ competitive research
  • User flow
  • Sketching
  • Wireframes/ prototype
  • Content strategy
  • User testing

Project Overview

Goal

To design a user-friendly platform that supports parents in understanding and nurturing emotional intelligence within their families, providing tools for self-reflection and guided learning.

Challenge

Parents often face challenges in promoting emotional intelligence due to a lack of accessible resources and tools that fit their busy schedules. Parental EQ aims to bridge this gap with a straightforward, supportive interface that helps parents on their journey to emotional growth and connection.

Outcome

A polished, intuitive app that enables parents to explore emotional intelligence concepts in practical, interactive ways, fostering a positive impact on family relationships.
